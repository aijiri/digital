Generative AI (GAI) at KUFS — Course Introduction

Welcome

This course runs for 14 weeks. Every week follows the same simple structure, made up of three parts: A. Watch, B. Workshop, and C. Weekly Learning Journal. Once you know the pattern, you'll know exactly what to do each week.

How Each Week Works

A

Watch

Start by watching the short video for the week. It introduces the topic and gives you the background you need before you begin the workshop.

B

Workshop

Follow the instructions to complete that week's hands-on task. This is where you apply what you watched, sometimes with a specific AI tool, sometimes without one.

C

Weekly Learning Journal

Answer the reflection question in your own words (at least 150 words) directly in the text box and submit. This is where you think about what you learned and how it connects to you.

Weekly Checklist

  1. Watch the video (A)
  2. Complete the workshop task (B)
  3. Write and submit your journal answer, 150+ words, in your own words (C)

Note: some weeks are AI-free by design (for example, brainstorming and hand-drawn prototypes). The workshop instructions for that week will always tell you which tools, if any, to use.
